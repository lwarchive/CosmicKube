hardcoded recipes:
- hydrogen + oxygen = water
- water + chlorine = tap water
- sodium + chlorine = salt
- water + salt = sea water
- nitrogen + oxygen = air
- iron + water = rust
- silicon + aluminium = feldspar
- feldspar + silicon = sand
- sand + water = dirt
- sand + sea water = beach
- dirt + water = earth
- earth + air = life
- life + time = age
- uranium + water = energy
- sand + time = rock
- rock + energy = fire
- fire + sand = glass
